About Us

Building Nigeria's Power Infrastructure

Your trusted partner in electrical engineering excellence

JEO Electrical Construction Nigeria Limited is a professionally managed electrical engineering company specializing in high tension and low-tension electrical works across Nigeria.

We are actively involved in the supply, installation, maintenance, repair, and commissioning of electrical power systems, including transformers, armored cables, and related electrical infrastructure.

Our expert services extend to the repair and refurbishment of damaged and faulty transformers, ensuring optimal performance, extended lifespan, and reliable power distribution for our clients.
