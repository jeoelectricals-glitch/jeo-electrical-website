About JEO Electrical Construction Nigeria Limited

JEO Electrical Construction Nigeria Limited is a professionally managed electrical engineering company specializing in high tension and low-tension electrical works.

The company is actively involved in the supply, installation, maintenance, repair, and commissioning of electrical power systems, including transformers, armored cables, and related electrical infrastructure.

JEO Electrical Construction Nigeria Limited also provides expert repair and refurbishment services for damaged and faulty transformers, ensuring optimal performance lifespan, and reliable power distribution.

With a strong commitment to safety, quality, and timely project delivery, JEO Electrical Construction Nigeria Limited provides reliable electrical solutions to industrial, commercial, and residential clients across Nigeria.

Our Vision

To be a leading electrical engineering company in Nigeria, recognized for delivering safe, innovative, and reliable power solutions that support national development.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality electrical construction, installation, and repair services by applying industry best practices, skilled expertise, and strict safety standards, while delivering projects on time and exceeding client expectations.
